Lightspeed onboarding

As Accesso will have key roll of your visitor experiance we made it easy to use with a super fast user opt-on process. Bring your visitor experiance to the next level with Accesso´s verify for access protocall today.

Designed to fit all needs

Accesso isn´t like no other verify for access solution. Its designed to be easy to implement anywhere, anytime. It can be used to open existing doors or be integreted with a gate access hardware unit.

Secure visitor verification

The Accesso´s verify for access process uses serveral high security steps to allow or decline the visitor access.
All user interactions needs be verifyed with Bank-ID, and the Accesso´s security protocall  

Why xPass?

Like no other system, Accesso can be configurated in many ways. The xPass units make this possible and is desiged to be used as a stand alone unit mounted on a wall or integrated into a visitor gate unit.

The xPass unit has integrated 3D camera for facial regonition or thermal health checking. Which it´s built in speeker and microphone you can communicate with your visitor.

Desiged to give you the freedom to define your pwn uniqe verify for access process.
